scrambled egg

egg are one of my favourite foods.here is a recipe for deliciously rich scramled eggs

ingredients

  • 2 egg
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

method

  1. melt bultter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. gently mix the egg and cream in a bowl
  3. once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds [as if you are making an omelette]
